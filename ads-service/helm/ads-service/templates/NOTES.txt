Prolance ads-service deployed!

Get the service URL:
  export NODE_IP=$(kubectl get nodes -o jsonpath='{.items[0].status.addresses[0].address}')
  echo "http://$NODE_IP:{{ .Values.service.nodePort }}{{ .Values.configMap.SERVER_SERVLET_CONTEXT_PATH }}/"

Check pod status:
  kubectl -n {{ .Values.namespace }} get pods -l app=ads-service

View logs:
  kubectl -n {{ .Values.namespace }} logs -l app=ads-service --tail=50
